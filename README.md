### Project Description
This application manages and tracks internship records for students. It allows users to log, update, and monitor internship details, making record-keeping simple and organized

### The Problem statement
Students often struggle to keep track of their internship details, such as company, duration, and tasks completed. Without a centralized system, important information can get lost, making it hard to showcase experience or stay organized

### The Solution
We provide a digital platform where students can easily log, update, and track their internship details in one place. The app organizes information like company name, duration, and tasks completed, helping students stay organized and access their internship records anytime.
